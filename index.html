Design System 01

Portfolio Website Hero

Built a clean personal brand hero with layered sketches, fixed navigation, and clear calls to action.

  • Problem: The first screen needed personality without becoming messy.
  • Decision: Use bold typography, a dark canvas, and visual artwork on the right.
  • Result: A stronger first impression that feels personal and professional.
Customer Experience 02

Support Workflow Cleanup

A sample case study layout for documenting customer issues, decisions, and service improvements.

  • Problem: Support conversations can get confusing without a clear structure.
  • Decision: Organize the flow around issue, action, outcome, and next step.
  • Result: Faster understanding and less back-and-forth for the user.
Business Website 03

Small Business Landing Page

A future-ready section for client website samples, useful for cold outreach and demos.

  • Problem: Many small businesses lack a clear online presentation.
  • Decision: Start with a simple landing page that explains the offer fast.
  • Result: Easier discovery, better trust, and a cleaner way to receive inquiries.
